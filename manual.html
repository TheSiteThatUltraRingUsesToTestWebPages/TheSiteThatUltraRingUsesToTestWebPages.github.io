MANUAL: For Sonic



Sonic uses a relatively simple control scheme, as the difficulty comes from the challenges the terrain creates.

You will need to master the controls to overcome them efficiently!



BASICS

MOVEMENT: Move Sonic by pressing LEFT/RIGHT. By holding LEFT/RIGHT, you will slowly build up speed.

Upward slopes will require more effort to climb, while downward slopes will increase your speed.



ROLL: While moving, press DOWN to enter rolling state.

You will have less control when you roll, upward slopes are harder to climb, and downward slopes give you even more momentum than simply running.



JUMP: Press any ACTION button to jump

In a jumpball state, much like the rolling state, you are able to smash enemies and TV montior power-ups, but watch out for projectiles!



VIEW: Look up/down by pressing and holding UP/DOWN.

Camera pans after a few seconds of holding the input. Must be at a stand-still, used to activate SPECIAL MOVES.





SPECIAL MOVES

PEEL-OUT/STRIKE DASH: Hold UP and press any ACTION button to rev up speed in place.

When you release UP, Sonic will shoot forward.



SPINDASH: Hold DOWN and press any ACTION button multiple times to roll and rev up speed in place.

When you release DOWN, Sonic will shoot forward in roll state.



INSTA-SHIELD/DOUBLE SPIN ATTACK: After a jump, press any ACTION button again to generate a shield around Sonic for a split second.

This allows Sonic to attack enemies from further away as well as deflect projectiles.





NEW MOVES AND TECHNIQUES

WALL SLIDE/JUMP: When jumping towards a wall, press any ACTION button to start sliding down the wall. Press any ACTION button again to jump off the wall in the opposite direction.

Chain multiple wall jumps together to access higher parts of levels!



WALL ROLL: During a Wall Slide, press DOWN to start rolling down the wall.

Useful for getting sliding under circular ceilings.



CHAOS DASH: Run fast enough for a few seconds to break the sound barrier!

During Chaos State, Sonic is way more agile, but you will need quick reflexes to keep it going!



CHAOS DRIFT: Double tap the opposite direction when skidding during a Chaos Dash.

Useful for quickly evading oncoming attacks if you have quick reflexes!
